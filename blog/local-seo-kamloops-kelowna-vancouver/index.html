Local SEO

The No-Fluff Guide to Local SEO for Small Businesses in Kamloops, Kelowna, and Vancouver

Local SEO is the process of optimizing your online presence so that people in your geographic area — Kamloops, Kelowna, or Vancouver — find your business when they search for what you offer. It covers your Google Business Profile, consistent business citations, customer reviews, and location-specific website content. For BC small businesses, local SEO is typically the fastest path to more phone calls and booked appointments.
