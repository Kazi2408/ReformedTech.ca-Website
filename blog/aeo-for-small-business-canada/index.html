What Is AEO and Why Canadian Small Businesses Can't Ignore It in 2026

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization — is the practice of structuring your content so AI tools like Google's AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity surface your business as the direct answer to customer questions. Unlike traditional SEO, which targets blue-link rankings, AEO targets the AI-generated summaries that now appear before organic results. For Canadian small businesses in 2026, being cited in these AI answers is increasingly the difference between getting found and getting skipped.
