Yes. All AI chatbots built by ReformedTech.ca are designed to comply with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), keeping your customer data collection legally compliant.
How do AI chatbots help BC businesses get more leads?

Local service businesses in BC — including contractors, clinics, consultants, and retailers — benefit most, as chatbots can book appointments, respond to quote requests, and convert more visitors into booked calls.
