Frequently Asked Questions

Are your AI chatbots PIPEDA-compliant?
Yes. All AI chatbots built by ReformedTech.ca are designed to comply with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), keeping your customer data collection legally compliant.
How do AI chatbots help BC businesses get more leads?
AI chatbots engage website visitors instantly, qualify leads automatically, and answer common questions 24/7 — so you capture potential customers even when your team is offline.
What types of businesses benefit most from AI chatbots?
Local service businesses in BC — including contractors, clinics, consultants, and retailers — benefit most, as chatbots can book appointments, respond to quote requests, and convert more visitors into booked calls.
Can an AI chatbot replace my customer service team?
AI chatbots handle repetitive first-contact questions and lead qualification, freeing your team to focus on closing sales and delivering service — not answering the same questions repeatedly.
